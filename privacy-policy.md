# Privacy Policy for Letter Blast

**Effective Date:** May 14, 2026
**Last Updated:** May 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Letter Blast ("the App", "we", "us", or "our")
handles information when you use the App. Letter Blast is developed and
distributed by Umar Muzaffar ("we").

We respect your privacy. Letter Blast is designed to function entirely on your
device. We do not run servers that collect or store your personal data.

---

## 1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Letter Blast does NOT collect, transmit, or share any of the following:

- Your name, email address, phone number, postal address, or any other
  contact details.
- Your precise or coarse location, GPS data, IP address, or device identifiers
  for tracking purposes.
- Your contacts, photos, microphone, camera, calendar, health data, or any
  other sensitive system data.
- Browsing history, search history, or activity in other apps.
- Any data for advertising, profiling, or third-party analytics.

We do **not** use any third-party advertising networks, analytics SDKs, social
login, or tracking services. There are no advertisements in the App.

---

## 2. Information Stored On Your Device

The following information is created and stored **only on your device** using
the iOS system feature `UserDefaults`. It never leaves your device and is never
sent to us or to any third party (except Apple, as described in Section 3):

- A **display name** of your choice (default value: "Player") used to label
  scores in the in-app local leaderboard.
- **Gameplay progress**, including your highest score, list of words you have
  already solved, leaderboard entries with timestamps, remaining arcade runs,
  and daily-challenge attempts for the current day.
- **In-app purchase status** — flags indicating which difficulty packs you have
  unlocked and whether you have an active "Unlimited Turns" subscription.
- **Settings**, including your selected age bracket (7–10 or 11+) and your
  audio mute preference.

If you delete the App from your device, all of this information is erased.

---

## 3. Apple Services

Letter Blast uses **Apple StoreKit** to offer in-app purchases and
auto-renewable subscriptions. When you make a purchase:

- The transaction is processed by Apple, not by us.
- Apple may collect billing and account information for the purpose of
  processing your purchase, in accordance with Apple's own Privacy Policy
  (https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).
- We receive only an anonymous receipt from Apple confirming that a purchase
  is valid for your Apple ID. We do not receive your Apple ID, name, payment
  card details, or billing address.

We do not use Game Center, iCloud sync, push notifications, or any other Apple
service that would transmit your data off-device.

---

## 4. In-App Purchases

The App offers the following purchase types via Apple:

- **One-time unlocks** to access additional difficulty levels.
- **Consumable** packs of in-game "turns".
- **Auto-renewable subscriptions** for unlimited turns (Weekly, Monthly,
  Yearly).

All payments, refunds, restoration of purchases, and subscription management
(including cancellation) are handled by Apple via your Apple ID. You can
manage and cancel subscriptions at any time via:

iPhone or iPad → **Settings → [Your Apple ID] → Subscriptions**.

We do not store your payment information at any time.

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

Letter Blast is rated for all ages and may be used by children under the age
of 13. We take children's privacy seriously and comply with the U.S.
Children's Online Privacy Protection Act ("COPPA") and equivalent laws.

- We do **not** knowingly collect personal information from children.
- All gameplay data described in Section 2 is stored only on the device and
  is not personal information under COPPA when used solely for App
  functionality.
- The App contains a **parental gate** (a simple arithmetic challenge that
  young children cannot easily complete) that must be passed before any
  in-app purchase prompt is shown for the "ages 7–10" gameplay bracket.
- The App does **not** include third-party advertising, behavioral targeting,
  contests, social features, sharing to social networks, or chat features
  intended for or directed at children.

If you are a parent or guardian and have questions or concerns, please contact
us at the email address in Section 10.

---

## 6. How We Use the Information Stored On Your Device

The information described in Section 2 is used only to:

- Show your progress, scores, and leaderboard inside the App.
- Track which difficulty levels and daily-challenge attempts you have
  completed today.
- Remember which in-app purchases you have made so we do not ask you to buy
  the same item twice.
- Remember your audio and age-bracket preferences across launches.

We do not analyze, profile, or transmit this information.

---

## 7. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information, because we
do not collect any personal information that leaves your device.

We may disclose information only if required by law, valid legal process, or
to protect the rights, property, or safety of users or the public. Because no
personal information is sent to us, there is effectively no information for us
to disclose.

---

## 8. Data Security and Retention

All data stored by the App is held in standard iOS `UserDefaults` on your
device and is protected by the security of your device and your Apple ID.
You can erase all data at any time by deleting the App.

---

## 9. Your Rights

Because no personal information is collected by us:

- **Access / Portability:** All your data is already on your device — you
  may view it inside the App at any time.
- **Deletion:** Delete the App to erase all data, or reset progress by
  reinstalling.
- **Opt-out of tracking:** The App does not track you. There is nothing to
  opt out of.
- Users in the **EU/UK (GDPR)** and **California (CCPA/CPRA)** have the
  rights described above. You may contact us at any time using the email in
  Section 10.
