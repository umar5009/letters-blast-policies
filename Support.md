Getting started
How do I play Letters Blast?

Tap or swipe the letters on the board to form valid words. Clear all required words to complete the level and progress to the next stage. The longer the word, the higher your score!

How do I move to the next level?

Complete all the word objectives shown on the current level. Once done, the next level unlocks automatically.

Does the game require an internet connection?

No. Letters Blast works fully offline. You can play anywhere without Wi-Fi or mobile data.

Troubleshooting
The app is crashing or not opening
Close the app fully and reopen it.
Restart your iPhone or iPad.
Make sure your iOS is up to date (Settings → General → Software Update).
Delete and reinstall Letters Blast from the App Store.
My progress was lost

Game progress is saved locally on your device. If you deleted and reinstalled the app, progress may have been reset. We recommend keeping the app installed to preserve your progress.

The game is running slowly or freezing
Close other apps running in the background.
Restart your device.
Free up storage space on your device if it is nearly full.
A level seems impossible to complete

Try forming shorter words first to open up new letter combinations. Some levels have hidden bonus words that are not required but help reveal the path forward.

Privacy & data
What data does Letters Blast collect?

Letters Blast does not collect any personal data. Your game progress is stored only on your device and is never sent to any server. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Is Letters Blast safe for children?

Yes. Letters Blast is a family-friendly word game with no ads, no in-app purchases, and no data collection. It is suitable for all ages.
